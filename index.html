WHERE WE WORK

Our mission is to transform hope, health, and resilience for all vulnerable children, youth, women, elderly people, and marginalized communities across Uganda.

We partner with government agencies (Ministries of Health, Education, Gender, and Agriculture), district local governments, and community‑based organizations to support vulnerable families in accessing healthcare, education, livelihood opportunities, child protection services, and emergency relief across diverse contexts and geographies. Our roots are in Uganda, where we have strengthened community well‑being in partnership with 22 district local governments and national ministries. More recently, our impact has attracted interest from neighboring countries, including Rwanda and Ethiopia, paving the way for future scale‑up of our community‑led development model across Africa.

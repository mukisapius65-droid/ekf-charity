Roles at
EK Foundation

Our mission is to improve health, education, and livelihoods for all vulnerable children, youth, women, and elderly people – and we rely on a diverse set of skills to strengthen, scale, and sustain the solutions that support them: community outreach and psychosocial support, child protection and case management, HIV/AIDS sensitization and reproductive health education, livelihood training and income‑generating activities, environmental conservation, emergency relief coordination, data collection and impact tracking, and government and community partnerships.

Product Research Manager

Deadline: 28th April 2026

The Product Research Manager will play a leadership role in shaping how EKFcharity evaluates interventions across the full lifecycle – from early experimentation and iteration to scale and long‑term sustainability and impact. Working closely with Programs, Monitoring & Evaluation, Data & Analytics, Technology, and Community Partnerships teams, the role will focus on understanding how, why, and for whom interventions work – with particular attention to context, equity, and impact on vulnerable children, youth, women, elderly, and marginalized communities.

Why choose
EKFCharity?

We’re proud of our diverse, multidisciplinary team, and the professional development opportunities afforded to each member – from on‑the‑ground community fieldwork to national policy forums and regional conferences. At EKFcharity, you’ll have the opportunity to do work that truly matters, alongside colleagues who share your passion for giving hope and overcoming adversity. We’re looking for collaborative leaders and problem‑solvers, with a vested interest in changing outcomes for vulnerable children, youth, women, elderly people, and marginalized communities in low‑resource settings across Uganda. Hear from our colleagues working in Central, Eastern, and Western Uganda.

Ensuring Safety in the Process

We understand the importance of safeguarding personal information and are aware of individuals who may falsely advertise job opportunities and misuse our brand. We urge you to exercise caution when sharing your personal information.


Please keep in mind:


All open positions with EKFcharity are posted on our official website, and link out to our verified application portal.
Official EKFcharity employee email addresses end with @ekfcharity.org; we will never email you from free web‑based email accounts such as Gmail, Yahoo, or Hotmail.
EKFcharity will never ask for money or other compensation as a term of employment, or to pay for application processing fees or for employment testing.
Job postings or employment applications that do not link to EKFcharity’s official website (www.ekfcharity.org) are not legitimate.

If you have any concerns or questions related to a job posting you found online, please get in touch.

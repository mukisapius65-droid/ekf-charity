Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for EKFcharity (Essien Kazibwe Foundation)
Effective Date: 2024-06-01
Last Updated: 2024-06-01
EKFcharity (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.ekfcharity.org or use our digital services (GROW, FIELDS, PULSE). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Who We Are

EKFcharity (Essien Kazibwe Foundation) is an indigenous, non‑religious, non‑governmental, non‑political NGO registered with the Uganda NGO Bureau (incorporated in 2021). Our registered address is: P.O. BOX 30565, Maya Masaka Road (12km from Kampala), Uganda. Email: info@ekfcharity.org

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data: Identity Data: Name, username, title, date of birth (for program eligibility). Contact Data: Email address, phone number (including SMS/WhatsApp), postal address. Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies (when you interact with our website). Usage Data: How you use our website, newsletter opens, donation history. Special Data (with consent): Health-related information (e.g., HIV status, pregnancy status) when you voluntarily use GROW’s helpdesk or participate in our health programs. We treat this as sensitive personal data and only process it with explicit consent. We do not collect payment card details directly – all donations are processed through Flutterwave (a secure third‑party payment processor).

3. How We Collect Your Information

Direct interactions: When you sign up for our newsletter, make a donation, register for GROW/FIELDS/PULSE, contact us via email or SMS, or complete a survey. Automated technologies: Cookies, server logs, and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) when you browse our website. Third parties: From our payment processor (Flutterwave) or referral partners (e.g., district health officers, community volunteers) when they enroll you in our programs with your consent.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes: To provide and personalise our services (e.g., GROW sends you health alerts, school reminders, or protection tips based on your profile). To process donations and send you tax receipts. To send you newsletters, impact reports, and updates (you can unsubscribe anytime). To improve our website and digital tools (analytics). To comply with legal obligations (e.g., reporting to Uganda NGO Bureau, tax authorities). To protect the rights and safety of our staff, beneficiaries, and the public. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (for GDPR purposes)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on: Consent: When you sign up for newsletters or use our sensitive health services. Contractual necessity: When you make a donation or register for a program. Legitimate interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and communicate with you. Legal obligation: For regulatory reporting.

6. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal data with: Service providers: Flutterwave (payment processing), Cloudinary (image hosting), email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp), telecom partners (SMS delivery). Government authorities: When required by Ugandan law (e.g., court order, child protection reporting obligations). Partner organisations: Only with your explicit consent (e.g., referral to a district health facility). All third parties are contractually required to keep your data secure and use it only for the specified purpose.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including: Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS). Access controls (only authorised EKFcharity staff can view sensitive data). Regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You share data at your own risk.

8. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law (e.g., donation records for 7 years). When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymise the data.

9. Your Rights (Uganda & GDPR)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights: Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold. Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data. Erasure (“right to be forgotten”): Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations). Restriction: Limit how we use your data. Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine‑readable format. Objection: Opt out of direct marketing or certain processing activities. Withdraw consent: At any time, for processing based on consent. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@ekfcharity.org. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate our website (e.g., session management) and optional analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand visitor behaviour. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some website features may not work properly.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are directed to vulnerable communities including children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

12. Third‑Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., Flutterwave, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. Significant changes will be notified via email or website banner.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions, complaints, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO): Essien Kazibwe Foundation Email: info@ekfcharity.org Phone: +256 751 545 436 Address: Maya Masaka Road, P.O. BOX 30565, Kampala, Uganda. For complaints regarding data protection in Uganda, you may also contact the Uganda Personal Data Protection Office (PDPO) at info@pdp.go.ug. By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
